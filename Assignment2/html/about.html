Our Story

In the heart of Invercargill, a small slice of Asia has found its home. Our lodge and grocery store is a labor of love, a place where the warmth of Asian cultures mingles with the rich flavors of the many homelands we represent. We invite you to be a part of our journey, as we've been sharing the diverse tastes of Asia with our community for several years now.

A Growing Legacy

Over the years, our lodge and grocery store have grown and evolved, just like the diverse Asian community we are proud to serve in Invercargill. We've become a beloved gathering place for Asians from various backgrounds, offering a wide range of familiar ingredients from across Asia, and providing a warm space to connect with friends and neighbors who share a love for their unique cultures. At the heart of our story is our unwavering commitment to you, our valued customers and guests. We're not just a business; we're a part of your lives, sharing in your experiences, and supporting you on your journey. As we reflect on our time in Invercargill, we are excited for the future and the opportunities it holds to continue serving this vibrant community with the rich flavors and traditions of Asia, and with the warmth of Asian hospitality. Thank you for being a part of our journey. We look forward to what the next chapter will bring!
